## 📺 Concept

You're operating a defense turret **remotely, through a TV feed.** That distance
is the whole game: your commands arrive with delay, error pop-ups flash across your
screen, and sometimes the mouse just won't go where you want it. Endless monsters
pour in from the right — hold the line anyway.

A literal take on the **"Losing Control"** theme: the player never has clean,
direct control, and surviving means adapting to that chaos.

## 🎮 Controls

| Input | Action |
|---|---|
| **Right Click** | Mark a position for the turret to fire bullets |
| **Left Click** | Mark a position for the turret to launch a mortar |

## ⚙️ Mechanics

- 🔫 Bullets and mortar fire
- 🕹️ Delayed, laggy controls
- ⚠️ Error pop-ups that interrupt the screen
- 🖱️ Random loss of mouse control
- 💢 When control is lost, the turret fires randomly and wastes ammo
- ♾️ Endless survival loop
